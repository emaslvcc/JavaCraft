In method movePlayer with parameter direction
    if direction is equal to "W" or "UP"
	    if player is not at highest point
		    move player up
	if direction is equal to "S" or "DOWN"
	    if player is not at lowest point
		    move player down
	if direction is equal to "A" or "LEFT"
	    if player is not at left-most point
		    move player left
	if direction is equal to "D" or "RIGHT"
	    if player is not at right-most point
		    move player right
