Function interactWithWolrd:

  blockType = the type of block at the coordinates of the player

  Switch (blockType)

    Case WOOD:
	Prints "You gather wood from the tree." and adds block to inventory

    Case LEAVES:
	Prints "You gather leaves from the ground." and adds block to inventory
	
    Case STONE:
	Prints "You gather stones from the ground." and adds block to inventory

    Case IRON_ORE:
	Prints "You mine iron ore from the ground." and adds block to inventory
    
    Case AIR:
	Prints "Nothing to interact with here." and adds block to inventory

    Default case:
	Prints "Unrecognized block. Cannot interact." and adds block to inventory

    End of Switch
    
  Waits user to click Enter
  
  End of Function
